"IRIS: Wisdom and Virtue" is an upcoming hardcore third-person action roguelike game combined with an open-world map to explore different paths and randomized puzzles to solve. The game is currently being developed using Unreal Engine 5.4 to achieve modern, AAA-style Western graphics and a smoother gameplay experience.

The current development team consists of three programmers, and we are actively looking for Environment Artists, Scriptwriters, 3D Modelers, Animators, VFX artists, SFX Artists, UI/UX Designers, Level Designers, etc. No prior experience needed, and students are welcome.

If you’re interested in collaborating with us, please contact “NICK_MC” through Discord, or join our official Discord server to learn more: https://discord.gg/hCQydCq

We are seeking opportunities to bring this game to life and ensure its success.
