HISTORY

September 2018

First Released Game

The first official game "Obby Master" was released on September 30, 2018.

Obby Master Trend

During the start of the pandemic period, the game has gained popularity for obby players. The community started to build up.

May 2020

August 2020

Obby Master Universe

The community was originally made for Obby Master fans and let them contribute in creating the sequel game "Obby Master | Revitalized"

Nick Games Founded

For better organization and future plans, Nick_MC decided to create "Nick Games" as the main community for all created games.

April 2021

October 2021

Released 5th Game

The release of "Noob Game" marks Nick Games' 5th official release.

Started AdvertBlox Campaign

Nick_MC started a Roblox game advertising campaign to help other games gain more attention by advertise it through each game.

March 2022

February-March 2023

The Talking NPC Trend

The first game that reached more than one thousand concurrent players. The game appears in videos by top YouTubers.

Non-Roblox Game Release

Powered by Unity, the game was created in 2022 by Nick and his team "Semak-Semak" to participate in a competition, ended up as one of the finalists, and then released in the Google Play Store one year later.

July 2023

April 2024

Rebranded to Nicknation

Nick Games rebranded to Nicknation Games, a decision to make it more searchable through many platforms.

WE HAVE MORE TO DO

We plan on finding new possibilities for us to grow the community and aim to build more high-quality games.
